Greenway Farms Quarry

The quarry at Greenway Farms was originally built by the Tennessee Valley Authority for limestone and other materials to build the Chickamauga Dam. The mines built near the area caused it to be slightly poisoned by sulfuric acid, which makes the water extremely clear, but also makes it uninhabitable for long-term habitats.

Photo taken at Greenway Farms, over the quarry.
