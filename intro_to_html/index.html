Who am I?

Hello! I'm Sam M. Weathers. In my free time I enjoy spending time with my family, playing guitar and singing for my punk band, skateboarding, and cooking. I work primarily in videography, photography, livestreams, and photo/video editing. I've worked in this field since I was around 12 years of age, shooting videos off of my hand-me-down Nikon, an iPhone 4, and iMovie editing software.

Bands I've Worked For

I've worked for several bands, such as Twink on Twink Violence, Laurel Hell, Medusa's Hairdresser, and many more. I'm currently the head of media for Laurel Hell, which is a smaller hardcore punk/metal band located in Nashville, TN.
